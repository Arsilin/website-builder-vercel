04 Principles

The four rules
we actually follow.

01
Show the build.

Screenshots, code, prompts, dashboards. Proof beats promises every time.

02
Share what cost you.

Tokens, hours, mistakes, dead ends. The losses teach more than the wins.

03
Build in public.

Half-built is welcome. Polished pitches without traction are not.

04
Ship, don't shill.

Promote the work, not the persona. Affiliate links go in the bio, not the body.
